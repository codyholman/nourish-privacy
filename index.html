Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cody Holman ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use the Nourish AI mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Health and fitness data: Height, weight, age, gender, activity level, calorie goals, food logs, exercise logs, and weight history that you enter into the app
  • Profile information: Display name and profile photo (optional)
  • Meal data: Saved meals, shopping lists, and AI query text

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: App features used, screens viewed, events triggered, and session information collected via PostHog analytics
  • Device data: Device type, operating system version, and app version

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Nourish AI app and its features
  • To personalize AI-generated calorie estimates and meal suggestions using your health profile
  • To send meal logging and weigh-in reminders (only if you enable notifications)
  • To analyze usage trends and improve the user experience
  • To respond to your support requests

How We Share Your Information

We share your information with the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Anthropic: Your AI query text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API (via our Cloudflare proxy) to generate calorie estimates and meal suggestions. We do not send your full health profile to Anthropic — only the text you type.
  • Cloudflare: Used as a proxy server to securely forward AI requests. Cloudflare may log request metadata per their standard practices.
  • PostHog: Used for analytics. PostHog collects anonymized usage events, device information, and your email address to identify your account in analytics. PostHog's privacy policy is available at posthog.com/privacy.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Data Storage

Your health data, calorie logs, and app settings are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. Your account credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain. Analytics data is stored by PostHog on their servers.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data stored on your device will be removed. To request deletion of your analytics data held by PostHog, contact us at the email below.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics data collection by uninstalling the app

To exercise these rights, contact us at codyholman91@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Nourish AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, we will send meal logging reminders and weight check-in reminders at times you specify. You can disable notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings.

Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information, including iOS Keychain for credential storage, encrypted API communication via HTTPS, and a secure Cloudflare proxy to protect our API credentials.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
